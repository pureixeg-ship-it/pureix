Our Story

Born from a love of
nature and skincare

Natural Soap started in a small kitchen with a big dream — to create soaps that are as good for your skin as they are for the planet. Every bar is a little piece of that dream, made with botanicals we source ethically and oils we choose for their skin-loving properties.

We believe that beauty shouldn't come at a cost to our health or our environment. That's why every ingredient we use is 100% natural, every formula is tested on willing friends and family (never animals), and every package is made to biodegrade.
